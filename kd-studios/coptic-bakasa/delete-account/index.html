Account and Data Deletion

App Name: Bakasa Coptic (بكاسة قبطي)

Google Play Games Services

This application uses Google Play Games Services for authentication and player profile management.

We do not create, store, or manage user accounts on our own servers. User authentication and associated account data (such as player ID, achievements, and leaderboard information) are managed by Google.

How to Delete Your Data

If you wish to delete your account or associated data, you can do so by managing your Google account through the following official Google page:

Manage Google Account

myaccount.google.com/delete-services-or-account
